Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Outlet Network?

    Outlet Network is an organization that aims to strengthen the creative community by unifying artists and creative workers into a single space. Our goal is to remove barriers from artists finding like-minded individuals with the hopes of inspiring and promoting their hustle. We aim to provide resources and recognition to smaller artists while sharing the encouraging and exciting stories of larger artists.

    Outlet Network’s presence is not limited to online. A large way of connecting and supporting artists is done through our events where our creative community comes together to network and support each other. We believe that physical interactions are crucial towards creating lasting connections and this brings real life impact.

  • Where is Outlet Network based?
    We are based in Toronto, Ontario, however we aim to spotlight artists from all over the world.
  • How do I get featured on Outlet’s page?
    If you’re an artist looking to be featured in our “Studio” Tour series you can send an email to Shauna@OutletNetwork.ca and Maria@OutletNetwork.ca with:

      - A small description of what kind of artist you are.

      - Photos and/or links to social media pages/websites that show examples of your work.

      - A description of your studio space.

      - Your availability.

    If you are looking to get featured in our street interviews look out on our Instagram page and stories to find events that we will be interviewing at.
  • How do I apply to create a collaborative event with Outlet Network?
    We are always looking for artists to create exhibitions with. To apply send an email to Shauna@OutletNetwork.ca and Maria@OutletNetwork.ca with:

      - A description of what kind of artist you are.

      - Photos and/or links to social media pages/websites that show examples of your work.

      - A small description of your vision for our collaborative event.

      - Your availability.

  • How do I join the team?
    We are not always hiring however we recommend sending us an email so that we can consider you for future positions. For work applications send an email to Shauna@OutletNetwork.ca and Maria@OutletNetwork.ca with:

      - Your resume.

      - What position you are applying for.

      - A description of why you want to work with Outlet Network.

  • How to apply for paid promotion?
    If you’re looking for paid promotion you can send an email to Shauna@OutletNetwork.ca and Maria@OutletNetwork.ca with:

      - A small description of what you're looking to promote.

      - Photos and/or links to your company’s social media pages/websites.

      - Date of project completion.

    Media package can be made available upon request
  • When is Outlet Network’s next event?
    The frequency of our events depends on the season and our availability. Keep an eye out on our Instagram page and stories for the latest updates on our upcoming events or send us an Instagram dm on @OutletNetwork.
  • Where is the best place to contact us?
    It is best to email us at Shauna@OutletNetwork.ca and Maria@OutletNetwork.ca as this allows us to see your message the fastest. You can also DM us on Instagram however there is a chance we will not see your message. Please allow for 1-2 business days for us to get back to you.
